What each % looks like.

Visible Light Transmission (VLT) is the amount of light that passes through. Lower number = darker tint.

70%
Light
Barely visible. Great for windshields and UV cut.
50%
Medium light
Subtle shade, still lets plenty of light through.
35%
Factory look
The most popular front-window shade.
20%
Dark
Strong privacy, you can still see out clearly at night.
5%
Limo
Deepest legal rear shade. Maximum privacy.

California law allows 70% VLT or lighter on front side windows. Rear windows & rear windshield can go darker.
